Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

The Global Recipe & Meal Planner is committed to protecting your privacy. Our platform allows for both public browsing and registered member participation.

1. Information We Collect

Guest Users: If you browse the site without an account, we do not collect personally identifiable information such as names or emails. We only track anonymized visitor counts.

Registered Members: To save recipes and use the community features, you must create a free account. We collect your Email Address solely for authentication and secure login purposes. Your password is cryptographically hashed and cannot be read by our team.

User-Generated Content: The recipes, ingredients, and meal costs you submit are public data intended for community sharing. Private messages sent to the administration team remain strictly confidential between you and the moderators.

2. Newsletter & Explicit Consent

Creating an account does NOT sign you up for our newsletter. We operate on a strict explicit-consent model.

If you wish to receive our newsletter, you must manually request it through the dedicated subscription box. Even then, we utilize a Double Opt-In system: you will only be added to the mailing list if you open the confirmation email we send you and explicitly click "Accept Request."

3. How We Use Your Data

Your email address is used only for the specific services you have requested (e.g., authenticating your account, or sending the newsletter if you explicitly opted in). We will never sell your email address or use it for anything you haven't specifically asked for.

4. Third-Party Advertising and Analytics

We use third-party advertising companies, including Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies (small text files placed on your browser) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using industry-standard backend infrastructure (Supabase). While we take all reasonable precautions to protect your data, no system is entirely foolproof. By using this service, you acknowledge that you do so at your own risk.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request the deletion of your account and data, please visit our Contact page to get in touch with our administration team.
