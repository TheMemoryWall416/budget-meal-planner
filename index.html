🛡️ Community Constitution

Welcome to our home. To keep this space safe, useful, and friendly for everyone, we rely on the community to self-regulate.

The Golden Rule

Treat others as you would guests in your own kitchen. Be helpful, be kind, and keep the focus on the food and the community.

How Moderation Works

You will see a ⚠️ Report button on recipes and comments. If you see spam, abuse, or a fake/expired deal, click it. We (Anton & Jenny) personally review every single report to make sure this stays a safe, welcoming place for everyone.

Note: Repeatedly breaking the rules will result in a permanent account ban. Let's keep this space awesome!
